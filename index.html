Consent Form for Visualization Explanation Assessment

Title of Study: Evaulating Explanation for Visualization Learning

Consent Form

What is our study about?

Our study focuses on evaluating various explanation strategies to enhance data visualization literacy. Initially, you must complete the screening process to be eligible for the main user experiment, which includes 19 questions about visualization. This study is concerned only with the screening process.

After evaluating your responses, you will be informed whether you are eligible to participate in the main user experiment. If eligible, you will receive a link to the experiment within four hours.

What will you do in this study?

If you agree to participate in our study, you will be asked to complete a pre-quiz, review learning materials, and complete a post-quiz. You will also be asked to provide feedback on the learning materials and your experience with the study.

How long will this study take?

We anticipate the study will take at most 30 minutes.

What risks are associated with this study?

Loss of Confidentiality: Data we collect, including question responses and user actions in our web application, will only be shared amongst members of our research team. Data will be deleted after our study. Research records will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law. Research records may also be reviewed by the Temple Institutional Review Board (IRB).

What are alternatives to participating in the study?

You are not under any obligation to participate in the study. If at any time you choose to end participation in the study, you may notify a researcher, and we will delete your data.

What benefits/compensation can you expect?

You will be compensated with $10 dollar worth gift card for participating in our study.

Ending participation in our study

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may end participation early at any time without penalty or loss of the benefits/compensation to which you are entitled. If you decide to end participation early, you may notify a researcher, and we will delete your data.

Other conditions of withdrawal

The PI may remove you from the study without your consent if the PI feels it is in your best interest or the best interest of the study. If you end your session and cannot reconnect, we will remove you from the study.

Are there any associated costs?

There will be no costs that you will be responsible for.

Who can you contact if there are additional questions?

If you have any additional questions or run into issues, you may also contact Stephen MacNeil, the PI, at stephen.macneil@temple.edu
